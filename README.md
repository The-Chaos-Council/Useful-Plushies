***

Plushy Powers
=

**So, plushies with uses you say. What exactly can they do?**

**They can do so much! Each plush has its own set of special abilities, but they all share the same four traits, or they can, rather. What are they?**

- **Power Storage**
- - If a character depicted as a plush is known for their technological prowess in their home franchise, they can hold a battery that can store RF/FE power.
- **Magic Power Storage**
- - If a character depicted as a plush is known for their magical prowess, they can hold either a bowl or a bottle to contain your magical power, like Botania's mana or Ars Nouveau's Source.
- **Power Generation**
- - If a character depicted as a plush is known for being easily distracted, they can be set onto devices known as Fidget Dynamos that can generate you RF/FE power or Create's kinetic stress.
- **Laziness**
- - This one's a bit of a nerf, but if a character depicted as a plush is known for being lazy, there is a chance their special ability will not proc, meaning instead of their power being used, nothing will happen at all.
